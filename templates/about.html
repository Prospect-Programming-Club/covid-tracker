About COVID-19

COVID-19 (Corona Virus Disease 2019) is a pandemic disease that originated from Wuhan, China. It spread throughout the world in early 2020. It was first discovered in November of 2019.
This disease is fatal as it blocks your lungs from absorbing oxygen, causing shortness of breath.

Ways to Stay Safe:

- Wear a mask at all times.
- Stay socially distanced from others.
- Wash your hands after every time you go out, for at least 20 seconds.
- Minimize attending or hosting events with a lot of people (4 or more).

For more information: visit Official CDC website
